Welcome to 1000 words!

A site where you can test your language skills

When learning a new language, learning the vocabulary is one of the most important parts. Learning the most common 1000 words is a great way to get a quick vocabulary boost.

The app uses a flash card mechanic to get you to test your ability. Use the text box at the bottom to submit your answer. Or tap the card to turn it over and see the translation.
